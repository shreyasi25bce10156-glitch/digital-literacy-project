Social Media Do’s and Don’ts for College Students

✅ Do’s
1.Think before you post, consider long-term impact on your reputation.
2.Use respectful and professional language in public posts and comments.
3.Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation.
4.Maintain privacy settings and protect personal data (location, phone, etc.).
5.Build a positive digital presence (projects, achievements, skills).

❌ Don’ts
1.Don’t share offensive, abusive, or discriminatory content.
2.Don’t overshare personal or sensitive information online.
3.Don’t engage in online arguments or trolling.
4.Don’t post during emotional moments (anger, frustration, etc.).
5.Don’t copy or repost content without giving proper credit.
