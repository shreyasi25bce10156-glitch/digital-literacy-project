Prevention Checklist 

Stay Safe Online – Checklist for College Students (India)

1.Use strong, unique passwords for every account (avoid using your name, DOB, or “123456”).
2.Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on email, social media, and banking apps.
3.Never click on suspicious links in emails, SMS, or WhatsApp messages (especially “urgent” or “offer” messages).
4.Do not share OTP, PIN, CVV, or passwords with anyone — even if they claim to be from the bank.
5.Always verify UPI payment requests before approving — receiving money does NOT require entering a PIN.
6.Avoid scanning random QR codes or accepting unknown collect requests on apps like PhonePe, GPay, or Paytm.
7.Use only official apps/websites for transactions — avoid third-party or unknown links.
8.Keep your devices updated with the latest security patches and antivirus.
9.Avoid using public Wi-Fi for banking or sensitive transactions.
10.Log out from shared devices and never save passwords on public computers.
11.Be cautious while sharing personal information on social media — scammers use it for targeting.
12.Regularly check your bank statements and report unauthorized transactions immediately.
